FREELANCE FRONT-END DESIGNER

My CV is at the bottom of the page

I am a hybrid of a designer, copywriter and a developer. I have made themes for WordPress and Drupal. I have written content for websites and I have designed some ads and features.

My main objective is to learn and grow, to have fun and to connect on an authentic level. For some clients I am a consultant, others ask me to work with them to refactor their css. Also, I do workshops. Big or small projects are not an issue, as long as we all have a good time. My clients include Intel, TamTam and smaller startups.

I believe in a good front-end architecture. No matter what technique you use, a browser will always end up rendering HTML and CSS. Having a good front-end architecture ensures the continuity in your project or your business. It would be a shame if you have to spend more time in restructuring and refactoring than actually progressing in your project.
